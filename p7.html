3D Model and Walk Cycle


About

This is a part of a project made for an assignment for RMIT University (Bachelor of Digital Media). The brief is to create a 3D character design and a walk cycle animation out of that character. I chose to create a puppet who comes from the future and has the ability to travel to different timelines to kill important people in the history.

Model Design

The design inspiration is from Vietnamese traditional water puppets and Vietnamese clothes like Non Quai Thao and Ao Nhat Binh. Since my character can travel through different timelines, it collects clothes of the people it kills and wears it to cover its puppet body and pretend to be a human. For the colour and styles, I was influenced by cyperpunk since it gives a futuristic feelings which suits the origin of the puppet. The colourful clothes also give the puppet a cute and harmless look which is to hide its killing intention.

The process of making the animation follows by these steps:

  • Character Modelling in Blender
  • Textures in Adobe Illustrator
  • UV editing in Blender
  • Add Texture to model in Substance Painter
  • Amature in Blender
  • Weight Painting in Blender
  • Walk Cycle in Blender
