Projection Mapping


About

This is a part of a group project made for an assignment for RMIT University (Bachelor of Digital Media). The brief is to create a projection mapping that matches the music of Xo Xinh from Monsoon Festival 2019 in Vietnam. We decided to create an endless run of a cube with abstract and colourful soundwaves; the sci-fi city. I was in charge of creating the last scene (highlight part) of the video in which the giant figure smashed the cube.

The Highlight part is the combination of all the stages with the change in foreground. It transits from the mountains to the city, where the cube would meet the final boss which is a giant nude model with cube head. The model is taken from online source as referenced below. It is already modified to be cube head to be suited with the theme. The content of the part is basically about the cube runs through the glitched stage and in the end got slammed by the final boss which ends the game. The final scene is the game over UI screen.

I made the city using particles system in blender. Basically I added a cube, expanded and added texture for it and then use particles system to multiply it to make it look like an abstract city. I also added a tunnel using cylinder and used array modifier to duplicate it.

Model

The animation of the model was taken from mixamo and the texture was made using shading in blender. Since the model is the final boss, I scaled her up as big as the size of the city. The final boss is another version of the cube but bigger and more chaotic. In the end, it got beaten by itself and therefore has to reset its own.
