3D Environment


About

This is a part of a project made for an assignment for RMIT University (Bachelor of Digital Media). The brief is to create a 3D environment on Blender. I decided to create a land where god and human used to live. However, due to an unknown reason, everyone suddenly disappears leaving the place abandoned. Over the time, the buildings and monuments deteriorates and are covered by bushes and flowers.

The city lies on top of the highest mountains in the planet which reaches even passes the clouds. From a far, the city looks like a giant ancient tower with heavily influenced Greek architecture. To get to the city, there are three pathways formed from also many high cliffs that are close to each other and make up a bridge to Valsia. Faraway from the city, there's a mysterious tower in a distance that looks like half of it was destroyed by great power.

I made the buildings and some of the cliffs on blender. Some of the cliffs and lands are taken from Quixel Bridge. After finish modeling, I moved the assets into Unreal Engine 5. I changed the composition after receiving some feedback from my lecturer.
